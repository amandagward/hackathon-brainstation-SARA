Severe Flooding Submerges Town

A catastrophic flood has engulfed, leaving large parts of the town submerged under several feet of water. Heavy and continuous rainfall over the past 72 hours caused the nearby Langley to overflow its banks, inundating homes, streets, and businesses.

- Langley Weather News

Weather Forecast and Further Risks

Meteorologists warn that the heavy rainfall is expected to continue for another 24 hours, increasing the risk of further flooding and landslides in surrounding areas. Authorities have issued a red alert, urging everyone to stay indoors or seek higher ground if necessary.

The community has shown incredible resilience, with many volunteers stepping forward to help their neighbors. Donation drives are being organized to collect clothing, blankets, and other necessities for displaced families. Residents are urged to stay updated on the latest announcements from emergency services and avoid traveling through flooded areas.
